Support & Resources

The organizations listed below provide support, education, crisis assistance, and resources for survivors, families, and individuals seeking help.

These organizations are independent resources and are not affiliated with or endorsing this website.

Human Trafficking Resources

National Human Trafficking Hotline

24/7 confidential support, crisis intervention, safety planning, and referrals.

Website: humantraffickinghotline.org

Polaris Project

Education, advocacy, survivor support, and anti-trafficking resources.

Website: polarisproject.org

Freedom Network USA

National coalition supporting survivors and anti-trafficking efforts.

Website: freedomnetworkusa.org

Find Local Help

FindHelp.org

One of the most useful tools available for locating free and reduced-cost services in your area.

Search by ZIP code for:

  • Food assistance
  • Housing help
  • Medical care
  • Mental health support
  • Transportation
  • Legal services
  • Financial assistance

Website: findhelp.org

Sexual Assault Support

RAINN

The nation's largest anti-sexual violence organization.

Provides:

  • Crisis support
  • Online chat
  • Resources for survivors
  • Information for loved ones

Website: rainn.org

Mental Health Resources

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Free and confidential emotional support available 24 hours a day.

Call or text: 988

Website: 988lifeline.org

NAMI

Education, support groups, and mental health resources.

Website: nami.org

Support for Children & Teens

Childhelp National Child Abuse Hotline

Professional crisis counselors available to assist children and families.

Website: childhelphotline.org

National Runaway Safeline

Support for youth experiencing crisis, homelessness, abuse, or unsafe situations.

Website: 1800runaway.org

Legal Assistance

Legal Services Corporation

Directory of legal aid organizations across the United States.

Website: lsc.gov
