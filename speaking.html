Why I Speak

My goal is to help people understand the realities of human trafficking, trauma, mental health, and recovery.

Through education and honest conversations, we can help young people, families, educators, and communities recognize warning signs, support survivors, and create safer environments.

I share my experiences to bring awareness, encourage hope, and remind others that healing is possible.
