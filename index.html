Welcome

Welcome to my website.

My name is Lea Marie.

I am a survivor of human trafficking, sexual assault, childhood trauma, and life-changing experiences that have shaped who I am today.

This space exists to educate, advocate, support survivors, and bring awareness to realities that are often misunderstood.

Human trafficking does not always look the way people imagine. It often hides behind trust, opportunity, dependency, manipulation, fear, and control.

My mission is simple: help others recognize warning signs, support survivors, and remind people that healing is possible.

My Mission

🎗️ Human Trafficking Awareness

Educating communities, parents, teens, schools, and organizations about the realities of trafficking and exploitation.

💙 Survivor Support

Helping survivors know they are not alone and connecting people with resources that can help.

🧠 Mental Health Awareness

Creating conversations about trauma, healing, recovery, resilience, and hope.

✝️ Faith & Hope

Sharing how faith has helped me navigate difficult chapters and find purpose through adversity.

Why This Matters

Many Victims Never Realize It

Trafficking often begins with trust, promises, opportunities, dependency, and emotional manipulation.

Recovery Is Complex

Survivors may experience trauma, grief, fear, chronic illness, anxiety, depression, and isolation.

Awareness Saves Lives

Education helps people identify warning signs before situations become dangerous.
